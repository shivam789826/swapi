# Swapi - Account Deletion Request

## Delete Your Swapi Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Swapi account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

### How to Request Account Deletion

You can request account deletion directly from the Swapi app:

1. **Open the Swapi App** on your device
2. **Go to Profile** by tapping the profile icon in the bottom navigation
3. **Tap on Settings** (gear icon in the top right)
4. **Scroll down** to the "Account" section
5. **Tap "Delete Account"**
6. **Confirm deletion** by entering your password
7. Your account will be permanently deleted Immediately.

### Alternative Method - Email Request

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion via email:

1. Send an email to: swapimail01@gmail.com
2. Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
3. Include in your email:
   - Your registered email address
   - Your registered phone number
   - Reason for deletion (optional)
4. We will process your request within 24-48 hours

## What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your Swapi account, the following data will be **permanently removed**:

### Immediately Deleted:
- Your profile information (name, email, phone number)
- Your profile picture and avatar
- Your posted product listings and associated images
- Your favorites and saved searches
- Your chat messages and conversations
- Your account preferences and settings
- Your device tokens and notification preferences
- Your location history and search history
- Your offers sent and received

### Deleted After Retention Period (30 days):
- Transaction records (for legal and accounting purposes)
- Reports you filed (for safety and moderation purposes)
- Backup copies in our database snapshots

---

## What Data Will Be Retained

Some data may be retained for legal, security, or business purposes:

### Anonymized Data (No Personal Identifiers):
- Aggregated analytics and usage statistics
- App performance metrics
- Market trends and category statistics

### Legally Required Retention:
- Financial transaction records (up to 7 years as per tax laws)
- Reports of illegal activity or Terms of Service violations
- Data required to comply with ongoing legal proceedings

**Note:** All retained data will be anonymized and will not contain any personally identifiable information that could be linked back to you.

## Important Information Before Deletion

⚠️ **Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.**

Before you delete your account, please note:

- **Active Listings:** All your product listings will be removed from the marketplace
- **Chat History:** All your conversations will be deleted and cannot be recovered
- **Pending Offers:** Any pending offers you made or received will be cancelled
- **Favorites:** Your saved favorites list will be lost
- **New Account:** You can create a new account with the same email/phone after deletion, but it will be treated as a completely new account with no previous data

---

## What Happens to Your Public Content

- **Product Listings:** Will be immediately removed from search results and public view
- **Reviews/Ratings:** Will be anonymized (shown as "Deleted User")
- **Chat Messages:** Will be deleted from both your and the other user's conversation history
- **Reported Content:** Reports you filed will remain for safety purposes but will be anonymized
